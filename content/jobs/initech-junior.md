## Key Contributions

- Designed, organized, and supported IT systems across office and manufacturing environments.
- Maintained operating systems and end-user hardware including laptops, desktops, and tablets.
- Supported business tools including Microsoft 365, Smartsheet, and Zoho.
- Contributed to entity-level disaster recovery planning (DRP) aligned with business impact assessment (BIA).
- Conducted proactive monitoring and maintenance to keep systems stable and reliable.
- Managed IT assets, inventory, software license renewals, and hardware replacement cycles.

## Responsibilities

- Provide daily IT operations support across systems, applications, and communication tools.
- Handle service and maintenance activity logging and execution.
- Build and maintain workstation standards for productivity and security.
- Coordinate with internal teams to improve system uptime and user experience.
